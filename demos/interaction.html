Perspective Hover

The position of the element tracks the cursor, and the dropshadow tracks inversely.

The radius r of object movement is proportional to the size of the object, given by the average of the width w and height h, such that r = ( w + h ) / 2 * f, where f is the factor of proportionality expressed as a percent. The radius of box shadow blur is equal to r, and its placement is inversely proportional to r. Thus, large objects will exhibit greater motion and softer shadows than small objects. Ideal values of f are between 4% and 10%; the default value of f in the examples below is 8%.
